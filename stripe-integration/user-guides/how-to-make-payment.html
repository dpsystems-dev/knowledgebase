How to Make a Payment (External Users)

External users (customers) can make payments directly from their customer portal. This allows you to pay invoices or make account payments at any time without needing to contact ABM staff.

How to Find Your Company​

What you'll do: Navigate to your company page where you can initiate a payment.

Step 1: Navigate to Your Company​

  1. Log in to the ABM Service Web Portal with your external user credentials
  2. Click on CUSTOMERS in the left navigation menu
  3. Select your company from the list

External user view showing company details and the MAKE PAYMENT button

How to Open the Payment Dialog​

What you'll do: Start the payment process.

Step 2: Open the Payment Dialog​

Click the MAKE PAYMENT button (green button) in the top right corner.

Make a Payment dialog for external users

How to Enter Payment Details​

What you'll do: Enter the amount and reference for your payment.

Step 3: Enter Payment Details​

Fill in the payment information:

  1. Amount: Enter the amount you wish to pay
  2. Reference: Enter a reference for your records (required) - for example, an invoice number
tip

Use invoice numbers as your reference to help match the payment to your account records.

How to Proceed to Payment​

What you'll do: Go to Stripe's secure checkout page.

Step 4: Proceed to Payment​

Click Proceed to Payment. You will be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page.

How to Complete Payment​

What you'll do: Enter your card details and complete the payment.

Step 5: Complete Payment​

On the Stripe checkout page:

  1. Enter your card number
  2. Enter the expiration date
  3. Enter the CVC (3-digit security code)
  4. Enter the cardholder name
  5. Select your country
  6. Click Pay to complete the payment
note

If you have a Stripe Link account, you can use Continue with Link for faster checkout.

How to View Your Confirmation​

What you'll do: See your payment confirmation.

Step 6: View Confirmation​

After successful payment, you will see the confirmation page with payment details including:

  • Payment amount
  • Reference
  • Confirmation that payment was successful

You can safely close this page after seeing the confirmation.

How to View Your Payment History​

What you'll do: View all your past payments.

Step 7: View Payment History​

Click Back to return to your company page, then navigate to the MY PAYMENTS tab to see your payment history.

MY PAYMENTS tab showing payment history for external users

The payment history shows:

  • Date: When the payment was made
  • Reference: The payment reference you entered
  • Amount: The payment amount and currency
  • Status: Payment status (COMPLETED, PENDING, etc.)
  • Receipt: Link to view the Stripe receipt

Payment Status Reference​

StatusColorDescription
COMPLETEDGreenPayment was successful
PENDINGYellowPayment is being processed
CREATEDBluePayment session created, awaiting completion
FAILEDRedPayment failed (see failure reason)
CANCELLEDRedPayment was cancelled by the user
EXPIREDGreyPayment session expired before completion

Need Help?​

If you encounter any issues with payments:

  1. Check the payment status in the MY PAYMENTS tab
  2. If a payment shows as FAILED, you can try again with a new payment
  3. Contact your account manager at ABM if you need assistance
